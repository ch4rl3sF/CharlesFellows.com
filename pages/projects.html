BurnBoss

  • Status: In Production
  • Started on: May 15, 2023

BurnBoss is a workout app, designed to let users customise their workout experience, allowing creation, editing, playing and deleting of workouts.
It utilises Google Firebase to store workouts to accounts.
I started working on BurnBoss for my A-Level Computer Science project, but it has stuck with me long after; not only as a project, but as an app that I use daily.
