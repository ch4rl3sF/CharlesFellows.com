Car Games Counter

Car Games Counter is a counter app built to reduce car sickness when keeping score of in-car games such as I spy or Yellow car.
Heather Mason and I built this app to solve our problem - keeping track of scores of games in the car took too much interaction with devices, which cause car sickness.
We used soft colours, large icons and text, and a simple layout to reduce interaction.
